Fried Cheese

Fast and crunchy fried cheese is delicious and fast to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 50g wheat
  • 100g bread crumbs
  • 2 x 50g slice of any fat cheese

Steps

  1. Put bread, eggs, crumbs in separate container
  2. Take one slice to wheat
  3. Put it into container with eggs
  4. When it's covered take it into crumbs
  5. Again put it to eggs
  6. Finish with another putting into crumbs
  7. Repeat for the last slice
  8. Heat oil in a pan to 350deg celsium
  9. When oil is boiling put one slice in it
  10. When it's light-gray take it out and repeat for another slice
  11. Serve with tatar souce
